Format Description

This course follows a flipped class format:

  • Pre-class material (recordings + readings) is assigned each week and consumed independently before the Friday session.
  • Friday is the in-person discussion and active learning session — not a lecture.
  • Monday and Wednesday are in-person lab and assessment sessions.

All graded deliverables are listed with approximate due dates based on the Friday of the relevant week. Final dates will be confirmed in Canvas.
