Learning Goals

  • Map each phase of the course — E1's measurement and uncertainty work, E2's prediction and signal conditioning work, E3's design and V&V planning work — to the corresponding nodes of the AMVF — CLO 3
  • Describe specifically how they investigated sensors, platforms, methods, or standards beyond the course-provided materials during the E3 design activity, and evaluate what the investigation produced — CLO 6
  • Articulate how their understanding of uncertainty, prediction, and validation evolved across the 15-week arc, and identify what they would do differently if they were starting Week 1 again — CLO 5, CLO 6
  • Answer, with structured reasoning, the question that opened the course: *How do you know this prediction is right?* — CLO 3
