E1 Week 1: The Modeling Spine, Measurement Literacy & Experiment Design

The course opens not with a lab but with a question: *how do you know this prediction is right?* Week 1 introduces the Aerospace Modeling & Validation Framework (AMVF) as the organizing structure of the entire course — a framework that also serves as a gateway to the engineering standards, handbooks, and validation resources that professional experimentalists use. Learning Goals

  • Locate measurement within the Aerospace Modeling & Validation Framework (AMVF) and identify the role of each node — CLO 1, CLO 3
  • Distinguish what a sensor measures from what it infers, and explain how installation effects and bias affect sensor output — CLO 1
  • Formulate a testable hypothesis and identify independent and dependent variables for their assigned canned experiment — CLO 1
  • Map a simple experimental workflow to the AMVF flowchart and annotate the diagram with their own design decisions — CLO 3
  • Locate and identify relevant engineering standards and professional handbooks as resources for experimental design, including ASME V&V 10-2006 as the primary standards anchor for this course — CLO 1, CLO 6
